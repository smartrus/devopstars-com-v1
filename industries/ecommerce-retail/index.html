PCI DSS: The Payment Security Baseline

PCI DSS is the payment security standard that any US company storing, processing, or transmitting cardholder data must comply with. PCI DSS 4.0 requirements map directly to DevOps controls: vulnerability scanning of all system components (CI/CD pipeline integration), change management for production systems (PR approval gates + audit logging), and network segmentation (Terraform VPC + security group configuration).

Our PCI DSS DevOps implementation satisfies Requirements 6 (vulnerability management in development), 10 (audit logging), and 12.3.2 (customized approach documentation) using pipeline-native tooling that generates evidence automatically - rather than point-in-time screenshots for your QSA.

Peak Traffic: Kubernetes Autoscaling for Commerce

US e-commerce traffic is not uniform. Black Friday, Cyber Monday, and flash sales create 10-50x normal traffic peaks that static infrastructure can’t handle without over-provisioning 364 days per year. Kubernetes with Horizontal Pod Autoscaler (HPA) and Cluster Autoscaler scales your application tier to demand within 2-3 minutes - and scales back down to baseline cost after the peak.
