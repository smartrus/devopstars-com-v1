Why DevOps Matters in the US Market

The US technology landscape has shifted. SOC 2 Type II is table stakes for B2B SaaS selling to enterprise buyers and institutional investors. HIPAA requires breach notification within 60 days for PHI exposure - and OCR penalties reach $1.9 million per violation category. FedRAMP is the mandatory path for SaaS selling to federal agencies. NIST 800-53 underlies defense contractor and government supplier requirements. And cyber insurance premiums are tied directly to your security and DevOps posture.

DevOps consulting USA isn’t a nice-to-have - it’s a competitive requirement. Companies that automate their delivery pipeline close enterprise deals faster (SOC 2), avoid regulatory penalties (HIPAA), satisfy federal procurement (FedRAMP), and operate with fewer production incidents.
