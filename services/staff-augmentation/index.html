A Senior DevOps Engineer on Your Team Within One Week

Hiring a senior DevOps engineer in the US takes 3-6 months. DevOpStars LLC embeds a senior engineer with your team within one week - for a sprint, a quarter, or as long as you need.

Duration: 3–12+ months Team: 1 Senior DevOps / Platform / SRE Engineer
The Challenge

You might be experiencing...

You need a senior DevOps engineer for a 6-month Kubernetes migration but can't justify a full-time hire - the role will be redundant once the migration is complete.
Your DevOps team is at capacity - CI/CD backlog is growing, the platform is getting fragile, and every engineer is firefighting instead of building.
SOC 2 Type II audit is in 4 months and you don't have a dedicated security/compliance engineer - you need someone immediately, not after a 90-day hiring process.
You just lost your principal infrastructure engineer - you need emergency coverage while you hire their replacement, without operations degrading.

The US DevOps talent market is one of the tightest in software engineering. Senior platform engineers are expensive, scarce, and in permanent demand. When you have a specific project - Kubernetes migration, SOC 2 preparation, CI/CD modernization - a 6-month hiring process for a role that may be project-scoped doesn’t make sense.

Embedded Engineers: Delivery Accountability, Not Advisory

DevOps staff augmentation USA from DevOpStars LLC is different from advisory consulting. Our engineers don’t produce recommendations and leave - they embed with your team, commit to sprints, get paged for incidents, review pull requests, and ship code. They’re indistinguishable from a full-time employee in practice, with the flexibility and speed of a consulting engagement.

When Staff Augmentation Is the Right Answer

Staff augmentation works best for: project-scoped work with a defined endpoint (Kubernetes migration, SOC 2 prep, CI/CD modernization), capacity gaps during peak periods or hiring freezes, coverage during a key hire departure or replacement search, and specialized expertise your team needs for a specific initiative but doesn’t need permanently.

It’s not the right answer for: building a permanent platform team capability, or replacing a fundamental DevOps practice that your team needs to own long-term. In those cases, we’ll tell you - and recommend a project engagement that transfers knowledge to your team instead.

Contact us to discuss your timeline, scope, and engineering team profile - we’ll match the right engineer within a week.

Frequently Asked Questions

What seniority level are your embedded engineers?

All DevOpStars LLC embedded engineers are senior level - minimum 7 years of DevOps/platform engineering experience, with deep expertise in at least two of: CI/CD automation, cloud infrastructure, Kubernetes, DevSecOps, or SRE. We don't place junior engineers as augmentation staff.

Do your engineers work US time zones?

Yes - all embedded DevOpStars LLC engineers are available during US business hours (EST/CST/PST) and overlap with your core team hours for standups, pair programming, and incident response.

How is this different from a typical staffing agency?

DevOpStars LLC engineers are part of our consulting practice - they bring NomadX institutional knowledge, standard practices, and access to our broader team for specialized support. We're accountable for delivery outcomes, not just headcount. We also provide clean knowledge transfer at engagement end, which staffing agencies typically don't.
